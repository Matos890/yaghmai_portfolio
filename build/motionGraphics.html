Motion Graphics

I can create explainer videos using motion graphics to make your project or business even more accessible and easy to understand. If you're looking to tell a story, this technique will undoubtedly make it more engaging and captivating. To produce these videos, I primarily use Adobe software, including Illustrator, Photoshop, and After Effects. Feel free to reach out for more information!

These videos are a trailer and the design conceptualization of the first episode of Mobi Doc, an in-progress ambitious web documentary centered around the almost untapped history of identity documents for the Institut Convergence Migration.
