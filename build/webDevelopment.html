Web Development

Let's dive deeper into what I can offer as a web developer, from delivering single-page applications to scalable design systems.

I can handle every phase of development, from design to deployment. Of course, the most critical work always happens during the design phase. Once I fully understand your requirements, I'll propose a design for your site, along with a web architecture that's tailored to your needs. Whether you’re planning to use a CMS or a static site generator, I can provide the support you need. My strength lies in learning new technologies quickly and diving deep into new areas of expertise, so I'm always ready to adapt to your project’s specific needs.

Tools and Technology

For front-end development, I primarily use JavaScript along with various associated frameworks, but I also have experience with Python. On the server-side, I typically work with Node.js and Express, while for databases, I’m familiar with both MongoDB and SQLite. Another approach I frequently take is building websites connected to a RESTful API, which is particularly useful if you plan to develop an app in the future.

Animations and Interactive Storytelling

Don’t forget, I can also bring your site to life with animations! I work with GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform), so if you’re aiming for a more interactive experience or want to create a site with engaging storytelling, I can enhance the navigation and user experience to make it both captivating and dynamic. Feel free to get in touch—I’d be happy to collaborate and build a website that exceeds your expectations!
